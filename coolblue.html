Project: Cool Blue

 

A case was purchased for £20 but who says you need an expensive case to build a really high spec machine? Enter cool blue with the following specs:

9800se >soft mod> 9800pro

2600+ athlon 333mhz fsb >overclocked> 400mhz fsb (hopefully)

550w 80mm temperature controlled fan psu

512mb crucial pc 3200 cas=3 ram

Abit NF7 V2.0

Onboard 6chnl audio

160gb 8mb cache hard disk

So far I have the case. 4 case fans and 2 cathodes are arriving tomorrow so I can begin the fun. next purchase must be the mobo if I can scrape the dammed cash...

The fans arrived and so did the cathodes, I fitted them and there extremely good for the price I paid. The fans are almost silent and give good air flow and are pretty. The cathodes are also good I got me dad to drill a hole in the front bay for their switch to turn them on or off.

I replaced the cases completely crap included psu for my beast 550w one. I gave my sister the old psu for her pc which should be in attendance at the last lan of the summer hols...

I had a bit of a time wiring the new psu up because I got bitten by a nasty insect while paint balling and my hand was the size of freaking football. Picture I uploaded doesn't show how bad it really was
