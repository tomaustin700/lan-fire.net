IDE cable rounding guide
Many of us have stupid thick airflow blocking ugly IDE cables in our pc cases, instead of buying new cables simply cut up some old ones with this guide!
1. find a clean'ish surface for cutting your IDE cable (I used a bread board!)
2. Get a sharp knife (hunter) and an old IDE cable (Victim)
3. Place the knife in one of the groves about 3 wires down and apply pressure until there is a small cut in the grove
4. Pull the body of the cable downwards and the cut section upwards and, vola! the cable separates (keep pulling until the smaller section is only held to the rest of the cable at the plug ends)
5. Repeat until the entire cable is separated into strands of 3
6. Bunch all the strands together and wrap with electricians tape or sticky tape
