Project: shuttle resurrection

 

It was a perfectly good sn45g shuttle, I took it to all the lan's and had a good go at all the other people carrying there 30lb rigs down stairs. Then disaster struck, it failed to post nothing appeared on the screen and it switched off after a few seconds all seamed to be lost...

For a long time it languished at the bottom of a dusty draw its components scattered and its thumb screws fretted away. But inside its north bridge a plan was forming...

As a last chance of desperation its owner gave it to an over clocker that had more hair than sense (and he didn't have much hair). The over clocker was berift of a case so improvised with a jumper for the power cord. It posted. Was this a fluke or had the shuttle come back to life, no one knows..

Lowly it looks, downtrodden mabye. without its case of lightly brushed aluminum and small psu. Instead it runs out of a small asus a7v8-x mobo box and runs off a bulky 550w psu. Although I don't have the components like gord had to get it posting I will soon...
